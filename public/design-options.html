Spellbook

Visual direction choices for the card search app
A: Reader Table B: Deckbench C: Night Binder D: Collector Focus

Four ways to make Spellbook feel more deliberate.

These are not full rebuilds. They are visual prototypes that keep the same core workflow: natural language search, commander colors, Scryfall results, saved cards, recommendation reasons, and card details. The strongest route can be applied to the real interface after you pick the mood.

Option A

Reader Table

Best when search should feel calm, fast, and premium.
Keeps the current light tone but adds stronger hierarchy and asymmetry.
Good default direction for the existing app with the lowest risk.
